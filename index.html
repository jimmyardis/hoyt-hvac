Our History

A proud South Carolina tradition since 1958

Our Founder: Hoyt Shuler

After serving his country with honor in World War II, the late Hoyt Shuler returned home with a vision to serve his community. In 1958, he established Hoyt's Refrigeration, bringing quality cooling services to South Carolina families.

With an increasing demand for air conditioning and heating services, Mr. Shuler expanded the business and changed the name in 1962 to Hoyt's Heating & Air Conditioning Inc. His commitment to quality service and customer satisfaction laid the foundation for the company we are today.

Over six decades later, we continue to honor his legacy by providing the same level of dedication, expertise, and friendly service that made Hoyt's a trusted name throughout South Carolina.
